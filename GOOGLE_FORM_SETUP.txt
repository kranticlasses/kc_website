# GOOGLE FORM SETUP INSTRUCTIONS

## Quick Steps to Connect Your Enrollment Form

### 1. Create Google Form
   - Go to https://forms.google.com
   - Create a new form named "Kranti Classes - Student Enrollment"
   
### 2. Add These 6 Questions (all required):
   1. Student Full Name (Short answer)
   2. Parent/Guardian Name (Short answer)
   3. Mobile Number (WhatsApp) (Short answer)
   4. Email Address (Short answer)
   5. Current Class (Dropdown: Class 6, 7, 8, 9, 10, 11, 12)
   6. Course Interested In (Dropdown: Foundation, Boards, JEE Preparation, NEET Preparation, Crash Course)

### 3. Get Entry IDs
   - Fill out your form with test data
   - Open browser Developer Tools (Press F12)
   - Go to Network tab
   - Submit the form
   - Find the "formResponse" request
   - Click it and look at Payload section
   - You'll see entry IDs like: entry.123456789
   - Write down all 6 entry IDs

### 4. Update Your Code
   Open: src/pages/Admissions.tsx
   
   Find line 29 and replace:
   const GOOGLE_FORM_URL = 'YOUR_GOOGLE_FORM_URL_HERE';
   
   With your form URL (change viewform to formResponse):
   const GOOGLE_FORM_URL = 'https://docs.google.com/forms/d/e/YOUR_FORM_ID/formResponse';
   
   Then update lines 35-40 with your actual entry IDs:
   formDataToSend.append('entry.YOUR_ID_1', formData.studentName);
   formDataToSend.append('entry.YOUR_ID_2', formData.parentName);
   formDataToSend.append('entry.YOUR_ID_3', formData.mobile);
   formDataToSend.append('entry.YOUR_ID_4', formData.email);
   formDataToSend.append('entry.YOUR_ID_5', formData.currentClass);
   formDataToSend.append('entry.YOUR_ID_6', formData.course);

### 5. Link to Google Sheets
   - In your Google Form, click "Responses" tab
   - Click the Google Sheets icon
   - Create new spreadsheet
   - All enrollments will save there automatically!

### 6. Test
   - Visit your website enrollment page
   - Fill and submit the form
   - Check your Google Sheet for the data
